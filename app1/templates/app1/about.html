Who We Are

The bridge between students and campus life

Student-Driven Platform

Society Connect is more than just a platform - it's a community-driven initiative created by students, for students. We understand the challenges of campus life because we live it every day.

Founded in 2022 at ARSD College, our mission began with a simple observation: students needed a centralized way to discover and engage with the vibrant society culture on campus.

Innovation at Heart

We leverage technology to solve real campus problems. Our platform combines modern web development with intuitive design to create seamless experiences.

From event discovery to society recruitment, we're constantly innovating to make campus engagement more accessible and meaningful for everyone.

Our Journey

From idea to impact - how we got here

The Beginning

March 2022

Started as a simple idea to help fellow students discover societies and events more easily. The first version was a basic webpage with society information.

First Launch

September 2022

Launched the first full version with event listings, society profiles, and basic search functionality. Gained our first 100 users within the first month.

Growth Phase

February 2023

Added recruitment features, improved UI/UX, and integrated with more societies. Student adoption grew to over 300 active users.

Current Day

2025

Now serving 500+ students across 15+ societies with advanced features, mobile optimization, and plans for expansion to other colleges.
